Q1 — Secrecy of the session key SK
==================================
Query (as it appears in phase2_authentication.pv and bpuf_a2k_full.pv):

    free SK_secret_test : bitstring [private].
    query attacker(SK_secret_test).

Meaning: a fresh private value is sealed under SK by the GSS
(out(c, senc(SK_secret_test, SK))). The attacker wins iff it can recover
that value, which requires recomputing SK = h(Si | Rj | Nu | N1 | N2).
Because Si and Rj are private and the nonces alone are insufficient, the
attacker cannot derive SK.

Expected ProVerif result:

    RESULT not attacker(SK_secret_test[]) is true.
