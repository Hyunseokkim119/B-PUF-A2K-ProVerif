Q3 — Injective authentication: UAV <-> GSS
==========================================
Query:

    query d : pid, n : nonce, sk : key;
      inj-event(UAVAccepts(d,n,sk)) ==> inj-event(GSSChallenges(d,n)).

Meaning: whenever the UAV accepts (after recovering SK from M5 and
verifying the tag), a matching GSSChallenges event must have occurred,
one-to-one. This rules out replay/forgery on the GSS–UAV link, where the
UAV authenticates via the PUF response R_j inside V_1.

Expected ProVerif result:

    RESULT inj-event(UAVAccepts(d,n,sk)) ==> inj-event(GSSChallenges(d,n)) is true.
