Q4 — Handover-ticket integrity: GSS2 <== GSS1
=============================================
Query:

    query pjbs : bitstring, sk : key;
      inj-event(GSS2AcceptsHandover(pjbs,sk)) ==> inj-event(GSS1IssuesTicket(pjbs,sk)).

Meaning: whenever the target GSS accepts a handover, a matching ticket
issuance by the serving GSS must have occurred, one-to-one. Because the
ticket is AEAD-protected under the private K12 (adec opens only a
ciphertext made under K12), the attacker cannot fabricate or modify a
ticket that GSS2 will accept. Hence the active session key cannot be
migrated by an adversary.

Expected ProVerif result:

    RESULT inj-event(GSS2AcceptsHandover(pjbs,sk)) ==> inj-event(GSS1IssuesTicket(pjbs,sk)) is true.
