Q1 — Secrecy of the session key SK
==================================
Query (in b_puf_a2k.pv):

    free SK_secret_test : bitstring [private].
    query attacker(SK_secret_test).

Meaning: the GSS seals a fresh private value under SK
(out(c_pub, senc(SK_secret_test, SK_key))). The attacker wins iff it can
recover that value, which requires SK = H5(Si | Rj | NU | N1 | N2). Si and
the PUF response are private and the nonces alone are insufficient, so SK
cannot be derived. The handover ticket carries SK under AEAD(K12) on the
public inter-GSS channel, but K12 is private, so this does not leak SK.

Expected:  RESULT not attacker(SK_secret_test[]) is true.
